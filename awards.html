Awards

Fellowships, honors, and recognition

2017-2020

Deans List

Linfield University

Dean's List honors are awarded to those students who have earned a GPA at least equivalent to cum laude (at least 3.650 GPA) in a given semester.

2016-2020

RC Hill Trust Scholarship

The RC Hill scholarship recognizes students for academic excellence prior and throughout their undergraduate studies.

2022-2023

Ohio State University Fellowship

The university fellowship program is designed to help recruit highly qualified students to Ohio State's graduate programs while advancing the overall quality of graduate education at Ohio State.

2026

Edward J. Ray Travel Award for Scholarship and Service

The Edward J. Ray Travel Award for Scholarship and Service enables graduate students across the university to participate in professional conferences to present original research. Unlike other programs that grant travel funds, the Ray Travel Award gives substantial weight to the applicant's service to their department, the university, and the surrounding community.

College of Arts and Sciences STEM Student Travel and Development Fund

The STEM Student Travel and Development Fund supports students in the College of Arts and Sciences by helping cover costs for travel and career development activities. The award is given to distinguished senior students to support their professional and academic growth.
